How It Works

The Cloud-Bridge Pattern

No local audio playback. Your phone does the heavy lifting while your car does the browsing.

1

Browse on the Dashboard

The car's touchscreen displays a beautiful Compose UI with your playlists, albums, podcasts, and personalized mixes — all fetched via the Spotify Web API.

2

Play on Your Phone

When you tap play, a REST API call targets Spotify on your phone as the playback device. Your phone handles all audio decoding and streaming.

3

Listen via Bluetooth

Audio flows from your phone through Bluetooth A2DP to your car's speakers — the same way calls and music already work. Zero configuration needed.

Features

Everything You Need,
Designed for Driving

Built specifically for automotive displays — large touch targets, glanceable layouts, and zero distraction overhead.

🏠

Smart Home Screen

A personalized dashboard with Jump Back In, Your Podcasts, Custom Mixes, Pinned Favorites, Suggested For You, and New Releases — all reorderable from Settings.

🎵

Custom Mixes

Daily Drive blends your podcasts with liked songs. Decade mixes (80s, 90s, 2000s, 2010s) are built from your saved tracks and Spotify recommendations — with 2×2 album art grids.

🎙️

Podcast Intelligence

New episode badges on your saved shows. Set any show as your Daily Drive news source. Full episode listing with one-tap playback and progress tracking.

🔍

Automotive Search

Full-text search across tracks, albums, and playlists with a 4-column results grid. Debounced input and large keyboard-friendly text fields built for driving.

📚

Full Library Access

Browse playlists, albums, artists, and podcasts with sortable lists, filterable views, and seamless grid/list toggle. Liked Songs always at the top.

📌

Pin Favorites

Long-press any playlist, album, or podcast to pin it to your home screen for instant one-tap access. Rearrange and manage pins from a dedicated screen.

🎶

Now Playing Experience

Full-screen Now Playing with blurred album art background, progress slider, playback controls, shuffle/repeat, heart/unsave, and Start Radio — all updating in real time.

📱

QR Code Onboarding

Add Spotify profiles without typing in the car. Scan a QR code, authenticate on your phone, and credentials are securely relayed via Firebase to the car in seconds.

👥

Multi-Profile Support

Family car? No problem. Add multiple Spotify accounts and switch between them from Settings. Each profile switch clears the library cache for clean isolation.

🧹

Clean Swapper

Toggle a family-friendly mode that automatically replaces explicit tracks with clean versions when Spotify has a matching alternative. Persists across sessions.

🎛️

Queue Management

View and manage your up-next queue with swipe-to-dismiss. Shows tracks, podcast episodes, and audiobook chapters with real-time progress on the current item.

🛡️

Resilient by Design

Automatic token refresh, offline detection, global rate-limit lockout, and graceful degradation. Built to survive spotty cell coverage on long drives.
